Classic Omelette Delight

Looking for a quick and tasty dish? Try this classic omelette! It's easy to make, customizable, and absolutely delicious. Whether you're in the mood for a simple meal or want to get creative with fillings, this recipe has got you covered. Plus, it‚Äôs ready in just 10 minutes!

Time to Prepare

Total: About 10 minutes

Prep: 5 minutes

Cook: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 large eggs
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or olive oil
  • Optional fillings: shredded cheese, chopped veggies, diced ham, cooked chicken, fresh herbs, or avocado

Directions

  1. Whisk the eggs: In a medium bowl, crack the eggs and whisk them well with a pinch of salt and freshly ground pepper. If you like a fluffier omelette, add a tablespoon of milk or water to the eggs.
  2. Prepare the pan: Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Once hot, melt the butter or heat the oil until it‚Äôs sizzling but not smoking.
  3. Pour in the eggs: Pour the whisked eggs into the pan and let them settle. Gently swirl the pan to allow the eggs to evenly coat the bottom.
  4. Optional fillings: As the edges of the eggs start to firm up, add your desired fillings. If you‚Äôre adding cheese, veggies, or meats, sprinkle them on one side of the omelette.
  5. Fold and cook: When the center is still a little runny, carefully fold the omelette in half. Let it cook for another minute or so to melt the cheese and heat the fillings through.
  6. Serve and enjoy: Slide the omelette onto a plate. Sprinkle some extra salt and pepper to taste, and garnish with fresh herbs if desired. Enjoy your meal!
